# 🏡 Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard** built using Power BI.
The dashboard analyzes Airbnb listings, pricing, reviews, host behavior, and market trends across major global cities.

It helps stakeholders understand:

* Market growth and trends
* Customer behavior
* Pricing strategies
* Host trust and performance

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to:

* Analyze global Airbnb listing trends over time
* Identify top-performing cities and property types
* Understand customer review behavior and seasonality
* Evaluate host credibility and trust signals
* Provide actionable insights for business and pricing strategy

---

## 📊 Dataset Summary

* Total Listings: **279,712**
* Total Cities: **10**
* Total Hosts: **182,024**
* Property Types: **144**
* Total Reviews: **5.3 Million+**

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Growth Analysis

* Tracks Airbnb listing growth from 2008 to 2020
* Peak growth observed around **2015**
* Growth slowdown during **2016–2017** due to regulatory constraints
* Impact of **COVID-19** visible with decline after 2019

---

### 2️⃣ Market Share by City

* **Paris, New York, and Sydney** dominate the market
* These cities contribute to:

  * Nearly **50% of listings**
  * Around **59% of total reviews**
* Paris leads due to relatively higher hotel prices

---

### 3️⃣ Pricing Insights

Average pricing by property type:

* Hotel Rooms → Highest pricing
* Entire Place → Premium segment
* Private Rooms → Mid-range
* Shared Rooms → Lowest pricing

💡 Insight:
Customers pay significantly more for privacy and full property access.

---

### 4️⃣ Ratings & Customer Satisfaction

* Best-rated cities:

  * Mexico City
  * Rio de Janeiro

* Lowest-rated cities:

  * Hong Kong
  * Istanbul

* Lowest scoring factors:

  * Cleanliness
  * Value for Money

💡 Insight:
Improving cleanliness and pricing strategy can significantly boost ratings.

---

### 5️⃣ Review Behavior Analysis

* Majority of users review only once
* **98.8% of users reviewed 3 times or less**
* One extreme case: A user posted **283 reviews** (possible anomaly)

💡 Insight:
Customer engagement is low—opportunity to improve repeat usage.

---

### 6️⃣ Seasonality Trends

* European cities (Paris, Rome):

  * Peak reviews from **April to August** (summer tourism)

* New York:

  * Peak activity in **November–December** (holiday season)

💡 Insight:
Seasonality strongly impacts booking behavior and pricing strategies.

---

### 7️⃣ Host Trust & Verification

* Majority of hosts are **identity verified**
* Very few anonymous or unverified profiles

💡 Insight:
Trust signals play a crucial role in customer decision-making.

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Airbnb growth peaked in 2015 and slowed due to regulations and COVID-19
* A few cities dominate the global Airbnb market
* Pricing is highly dependent on property type
* Customer engagement (reviews) is low and mostly one-time
* Seasonality significantly impacts bookings
* Trust and verification increase platform reliability
